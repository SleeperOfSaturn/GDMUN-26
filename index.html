This year, across seven vibrant committees, we push the boundaries even further. We invite you to step into an arena that will test your convictions and sharpen your resolve. Bold discourse is not for the faint-hearted; it is for the audacious, the relentless, and the fearless. The secretariat looks forward to a defining edition of GDMUN. The only question remains: will you conquer the floor, or be silenced by it?
